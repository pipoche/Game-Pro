    Icefield is a simple and well-known mini game. The purpose is to walk through the icefield avoiding falling into the ice. Each successful step increases the amount of winnings. There is only one loss-making tray in each row
    HAVE A LUCKY TRY!
    You can choose the sizes of the field : 2x3 ; 3x6 ; 4x9 ; 5x12 ; 6x15. To do so, you can choose preferred field from the base game
    After choosing the size of the field, select the size of the bet and click “Place Bet” button
    Walk through the icefield
    Click any tray in the highlighted row to choose your next move on the icefield
    In case of successful opening of the column, the winning is determined according to the following rule: Bet amount multiple by the coefficient at the top of the successfully opened column.
    Please be aware that any malfunctions during gameplay will result in the voiding of all winnings and bets.
    If your chosen place is safe - you win!
    If there was a loss-making tray in the row, then you lose your bet and all the winnings in this round
    You can cash out at any time by clicking “Cashout” button
    Also you can take risk and proceed to win more!
    A change in the number of fields leads to a change in the coefficients of the column
